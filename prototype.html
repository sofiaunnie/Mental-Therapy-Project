Our Approach

Like many modern therapists, We use a combination of approaches to suit my clients unique needs. Our background is in psychodynamic pyschotherapy and a treatment called Accelerated Experiment Dynamic pyschotherapy(AEDP). You might not be familiar with this treatment. So let us tell you about it.

While many therapeutic approaches have roots in looking at what is not working or 'wrong' AEDP pulls for your strengths and your natural ability to heal.

  • It focuses us in-the-moment during each session. We will look for cues from your body to help you express an emotion that may have been stuck for a while.

  • Looking at what is going on with you right now allows us to bring out inner abiities to heal.

  • A focus on connection and attachment facilities the repair of old pattrns and ways of relating that may not be working anymore.
